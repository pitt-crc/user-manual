# Data Management

This section covers working with your data on CRCD: **where it lives**, **who
can access it**, and **how to move it** in and out.

Start with [File Systems](file-systems.md) to choose the right storage for your
work — home, high-performance, and standard-tier project space each have
different quotas and performance. Use [File Permissions and
Ownership](file-permissions.md) to control access and share data with your group
or collaborators, and [File Transfer](file-transfer-methods/index.md) to move
data between your computer, the clusters, and cloud storage.
