# Getting an Account

Access to CRCD computing resources requires an active account. This section covers how to request and manage your CRCD account.

## Who Has Access?

The [CRCD-P3: Open Science Research Computing and Data Environment Usage Policy](https://crc.pitt.edu/sites/default/files/assets/CRC%20P3%20Open%20Science%20Research%20Computing%20and%20Data%20Environment%20Usage%20Policy.pdf) defines the scope of access.

CRCD resources are available to:

- All Pitt faculty, instructors, Emeritus faculty, and center directors
- Students, postdoctoral fellows, and staff sponsored by eligible PIs
- External collaborators and alumni through the [Sponsored Account](../getting-started/sponsored_account.md) mechanism

## How to Request an Account

1. **Faculty and PIs**: Submit an account request at [crc.pitt.edu](https://crc.pitt.edu/getting-started)
2. **Students and Staff**: Your PI must request access on your behalf through their allocation
3. **External Collaborators**: See [Sponsored Accounts](../getting-started/sponsored_account.md)

## Prerequisites

Before connecting to CRCD systems, ensure you have:

- An active Pitt username and password
- [Pitt VPN (PulseSecure/GlobalProtect)](https://services.pitt.edu/TDClient/33/Portal/KB/ArticleDet?ID=293) installed and connected when off-campus
- Multi-factor authentication (Duo) configured for your Pitt account

!!! note
    All connections to CRCD systems require the Pitt VPN when connecting from off-campus networks.
