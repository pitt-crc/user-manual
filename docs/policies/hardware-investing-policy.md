# Hardware Investing Policy

CRCD provides a hardware investment opportunity for researchers. Rather than purchasing, installing, and maintaining their own hardware, researchers can allocate grant or startup funds to CRCD for community hardware purchases.

## Benefits of Investing

| Benefit | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Professional Maintenance | All hardware is managed by the [Network Operations Center](https://www.technology.pitt.edu/network-operations-center-noc) at no charge, with state-of-the-art networking and security |
| Slurm Allocation SUs | 5-year investment allocation equivalent to 100% of the computational resources the funds would have provided |
| Bulk Volume Pricing | Lower equipment prices negotiated by CRCD based on large-volume cluster purchases |
| Better Utilization | Shared pooling increases overall utilization and reduces energy consumption compared to individual lab hardware |

## How Investment Resources Are Made Available

The bulk of invested hardware is made available to the broader Pitt research community. Investors can maintain exclusive access to up to **20 percent** of their purchased hardware.

**Example:** If a faculty member invests in 5 compute nodes:

- **4 nodes** are available to the community (including the investor)
- **1 node** can be set aside exclusively for the investor's group

!!! note
    Exclusive investment hardware is housed within the cluster and is subject to downtime during quarterly maintenance.

## Service Unit Guarantee

The investor receives **100 percent of the equivalent service units** from the contributed funds for 5 years (the expected hardware lifetime). If not fully expended, the SUs remain available for an additional 2 years. The allocation is separate from any existing proposal or standard allocation.
